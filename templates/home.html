ABOUT THE DISEASE

CORONA-VIRUS

Corona Virus also termed as COVID-19 is a disease that spreads by touch and coming in contact with an infected person. This illness can damage your lungs and airways permanently and also reduce your immunity. It was discovered in December 2019 in Wuhan, Hubei, China.

Some common symptoms of Covid19 are high fever, dry cough, shortness of breath and breathing difficulties. It can be severe for people with low immunity as it can cause pneumonia, severe acute respiratory syndromes, kidney failure and can be fatal.

HOW CORONAVIRUS IS SPREAD?

TRANSMISSION OF COVID-19

The Corona Virus spreads mostly through touch and we should mainatin social distancing. Below are some common transmission methods of infection.

Person-to-person spread as close contact with infected

The Corona Virus is mainly spread when we come in contact with a person who's already affected by Covid-19. Hence, social distancing is recommended for everyone.

Touching or contact with infected surfaces or objects

A person can be affected by COVID-19 if they touch infected objects like building elevators. It is advised to sanitize your hands everytime you touch such surfaces.

Coming in contact with a person who has cough

The most common symptoms of Covid-19 are cough and fever. It is advisable to not come in physical contact with such infected people and maintain social distancing.

HOW WHAT ARE THE SYMPTOMS OF COVID-19?

SYMPTOMS OF CORONAVIRUS

The most common symptoms of COVID-19 are fever, mild cough and fatigue. Some patients may have joint aches and pains and may lose the sensation of taste and smell. These symptoms are exposed within 2-14 days after getting infected by Covid-19

HOW TO PROTECT YOURSELF?

PREVENTION & ADVICE

It is advised to take the vaccine immediately and also its important for patients with diabetes and high blood pressure to take it as soon as possible. Seek medical help whenever required and stay home as much as you can.

Wash your hands frequently

Wash your hands frequently and especially when you come home and always keep a pocket sanitizer with you and santize your hands often.

Maintain social distancing

Maintain minimum 3 feet distance between yourself & anyone who is around you. If you are too close, you may get infected.

Avoid touching face

Do not touch external surfaces or objects at public places and then rub your face, nose or mouth as it may lead to Covid19.

Practice respiratory hygiene

Always cover your mouth while coughing or sneezing and wear a mask all time when in public places to avoid the infection.
